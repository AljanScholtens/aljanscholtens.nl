When I was 15 years old I started taking photos with an old Pentax SLR of my father. After that I bought my first DSLR and within a few years my interest in landscape photography has grown. Especially 2016 was the year when I started to develop myself more as a landscape photographer.

Most of the time I'm taking photos in the Netherlands and frequently I travel to other countries as well. I always bring my Canon 6D, Canon 24mm F2.8 IS USM, Canon 70-200mm F4L IS USM, Benro TMA37C tripod and some Nisi Filters with me.

If you want to hire me as a photographer, contact me at aljan@scholtens.me. You can also hire me as a drone photographer/videographer. For more inspiration and frequent work, follow me on Instagram.
