At the age of 15 I started taking photos with an old Pentax SLR from my father. Soon after that I bought my first DSLR, within a few years my interest in landscape photography grew and especially 2015 was the start of much more landscape photography.

Most of the time I'm taking photos in the Netherlands and frequently I travel to other countries. I always bring my Canon 6D, Canon 24mm F2.8 IS USM, Canon 70-200mm F4L IS USM, Induro CLT303L tripod, Nisi Filters and my dog Nikki.

If you want to hire me, contact me at aljan@scholtens.me.
